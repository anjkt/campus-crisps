Frequently Ask Questions
  • What is the Crisps Campus Ambassador Program about?
    Crisps Campus Ambassador Program is an exclusive opportunity to learn new skills, build self confidence and get rewarded for it! Our aim is to enhance information making it more effortless using Crisps and further help in setting up aware mindsets in campuses.
  • Who can apply?
    Anyone who meets the below criteria:
    • Minimum 18 years of age
    • Graduate and post-graduate students, currently pursuing their degree
  • How do I apply? What happens after I apply?
    You can apply directly through our website using the ‘Apply Now’ button on this page. Once you apply, you will hear from us if you are selected.
  • Can students from any college/university apply?
    Yes, students from any college & university can apply for the Crisps Campus Ambassador Program
  • How long is the Campus Ambassador Program?
    The program is for a duration of 12 months.
  • What are the responsibilities of a campus ambassador?
    As a Campus Ambassador for Crisps, you are required to do the following:
    • Create a buzz about Crisps in your campus & create awareness about Crisps information solution in your campus
    • Be the anchor for communications between Crisps and the students of respective campus
    • Educate the student community about the benefits/usage/services offered by Crisps free of cost
    • Use posters, social media, student community and other creative ideas to publicize Crisps
    • Push marketing activities and drive transactions in your campus
    • Provide content – articles, videos, testimonial etc. for student community
    • Taking up the initiative of on-boarding educational institutions for campaign
  • Will Crisps support the ambassadors in campaigns and activities they execute?
    Yes, Crisps will provide adequate resources and guidance for successful execution of planned marketing activities.
  • Are there any incentives for being a student ambassador?
    Yes, this is your opportunity to develop strategic, communication, leadership skills and get rewarded for it. Apart from learnings that will benefit your career, you will also get performance based cash incentive, certificate on completion of the program and other rewards like rockstar certificate for high performing campus ambassador. You may stand a chance for pre-placement interview based on your performance.
  • Who can I contact if I have any questions regarding the Campus Ambassador Program?
    You can contact us at contact@Crisps.tech.
